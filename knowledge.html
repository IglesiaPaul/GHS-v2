What exactly is hemp?

A versatile crop used in textiles, construction, nutrition, and biocomposites—thriving with fewer inputs and improving soils.

Botany

Fast growth, deep roots, and strong fibers make unique material properties possible.

Applications

Hempcrete, textiles, bioplastics, nutrition, cosmetics—each with distinct value chains.

Sustainability

Lower water use, potential for soil carbon, and durable products that store carbon.
